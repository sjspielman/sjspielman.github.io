About Me

I recently obtained my Ph.D. from the Ecology, Evolution, and Behavior graduate program at UT Austin, through Claus Wilke's lab. In August 2016, I will be joining the Institute for Genomics and Evolutionary Medicine at Temple University as a Research Assistant Professor in Dr. Sergei Kosakovsky Pond's lab. My research interests broadly encompass computational molecular evolution, phylogenetics, and protein evolution.
