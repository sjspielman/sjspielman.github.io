I recently obtained my Ph.D. from the Ecology, Evolution, and Behavior graduate program at UT Austin, through Claus Wilke's lab. In August 2016, I will be joining the Institute for Genomics and Evolutionary Medicine at Temple University as a Postdoctoral Researcher with Dr. Sergei Pond.
