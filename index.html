    About Me

    I am a 4th year PhD Candidate in Claus Wilke's lab through the Ecology, Evolution, and Behavior graduate program at UT Austin. You can contact me at stephanie.spielman@gmail.com, and you can access my CV here (updated 11/14/14). My research interests broadly encompass computational molecular evolution, protein evolution, and phylogenetics, with a specific focus on models of coding sequence evolution.
