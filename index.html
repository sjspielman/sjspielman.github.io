About Me


{% include image.html url="/images/me.png" width=145 align="left" %}

I am a Research Assisant Professor in the Institute for Genomics and Evolutionary Medicine at Temple University, in Dr. Sergei Kosakovsky Pond's lab. I received my Ph.D. from the Ecology, Evolution, and Behavior graduate program at UT Austin, through Claus Wilke's lab. My research interests broadly encompass computational molecular evolution, phylogenetics, and protein evolution.
