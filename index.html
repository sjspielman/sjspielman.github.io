About Me


{% include image.html url="/images/me.png" width=145 align="left" %}

I am a Research Assisant Professor in the Institute for Genomics and Evolutionary Medicine at Temple University , in Sergei L. Kosakovsky Pond's lab. I received my Ph.D. from the Ecology, Evolution, and Behavior graduate program at UT Austin, through Claus Wilke's lab. My research interests broadly encompass computational molecular evolution, phylogenetics, and comparative genomics. In addition to research, I have a strong interest in teaching and undergraduate education.
